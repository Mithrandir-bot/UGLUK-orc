## Token Economics

### Why This Works

1. **Friction removal** - Gas-free swaps attract traders
2. **Reward alignment** - Holders benefit from volume (not just holders)
3. **Community incentives** - Bigger Horde = bigger rewards
4. **Sustainable** - Rewards come from trading fees, not new supply

### Anti-Dumping

- Rewards concentrate in active traders
- Tier system encourages holding
- DAO can vote to adjust reward rates
- Treasury locked for community benefit
